Projects I've built
Bosques Verificados
Wood supply chain traceability for EUDR compliance
2025

Built solo — a full traceability system for the wood supply chain, designed to meet European Union Deforestation Regulation requirements. Producers register products linked to their land plots, and the system uses WHISP (Open Foris) for geospatial verification of forest existence.

  • Smart contract architecture inspired by EPCIS (GS1) for event recording across the full supply chain — creation, transformation, transfers
  • Queryable ancestry system to trace any final product back to its origin land and full event history
  • Deforestation risk scoring associated with each product based on geospatial data
  • Separate smart contract for document integrity verification via on-chain hash storage

Avaldao
Decentralized mutual guarantees platform
2022 — present

A platform that enables small businesses (PyMEs) to access decentralized guarantees on-chain. Originally started in 2022, the rapid evolution of the blockchain ecosystem required a full rebuild with current tooling. The new dApp is deployed on Rootstock and designed for EVM compatibility.

  • Authentication via wallet message signing combined with email/password flows
  • Smart contract interaction through Reown AppKit with transaction state management
  • New contracts being developed with full test coverage to prevent production issues found in the original deployment
  • Role-based access with per-network caching in MongoDB
Next.js Solidity Reown AppKit Hardhat MongoDB Rootstock / EVM
ForestMaker
Real-world asset tokenization for reforestation
2023

A platform enabling investors to participate in forestry projects through tokenized assets. Each reforestation project issues its own ERC-20 extended tokens. Investors purchase tokens representing their stake and can claim periodic interest payments.

  • Custom ERC-20 token extensions built with OpenZeppelin — including a debt token implementing CMTAT standards with interest rate distribution at cutoff dates
  • Extensive smart contract testing that uncovered and resolved critical issues pre-launch
  • Token deployment orchestrated from a .NET backend
  • Flutter mobile app for token purchase and interest claiming, plus a Next.js dApp under development at dapp.forestmaker.org
  • Angular backoffice for project administration
Solidity OpenZeppelin Hardhat Flutter Next.js Angular .NET
